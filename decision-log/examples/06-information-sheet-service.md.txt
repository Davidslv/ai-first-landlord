## Why this entry's discipline matters

The Information Sheet 2026 was a **one-off existing-tenant rollout** with a hard 31 May 2026 statutory deadline. Service evidence is the file record; the audit trail is the only defence against a civil-penalty challenge.

Three load-bearing details:

- **Version-pinning the gov.uk PDF.** The published version of the Information Sheet may be updated by gov.uk between publication and service. Filing the dated version (e.g. *info-sheet-2026-gov-uk-20-03-2026.pdf*) shows which version was served on which date.
- **Read-receipt timestamp.** Email service plus read-receipt is acceptable evidence of service for landlords whose tenancy agreements name email as the preferred channel. Posted recorded delivery is the fallback where email is not the agreed channel.
- **Cross-reference to the dashboard.** The compliance dashboard's INFO SHEET column flips to *served* and carries the historical record. The decision-log entry plus dashboard plus TOM line 19 plus the version-pinned PDF make a four-piece audit trail.

## The same discipline applies to Written Statement of Terms

For new tenancies granted on or after 1 May 2026, the equivalent decision-log entry is logged under the same DOCS category but cites the Written Statement of Terms (s.16D HA 1988 as inserted by RRA 2025) — different statutory anchor, different cohort, different timing rule (served before signing, typically incorporated as a schedule to the agreement).

The Written Statement entry differs from the above in three places:

- **Decision:** *Serve the Written Statement of Terms (s.16D HA 1988) on [APPLICANT_REF] at [PROPERTY_REF] as Schedule [A] to the tenancy agreement, with signed acknowledgement on the signature page.*
- **Rationale:** cites s.16D HA 1988 as inserted by RRA 2025; same penalty band; signed acknowledgement on the signature page is the load-bearing evidence (stronger than email read-receipt alone).
- **Outcome:** records the date the agreement was countersigned and the read-receipt timestamp on the cover email — both retained as evidence of service.

The two cohorts are **not interchangeable**. Information Sheet for tenancies in place before 1 May 2026; Written Statement of Terms for tenancies granted on or after.
