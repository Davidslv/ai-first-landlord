## Field-by-field guidance

### DECISION
One sentence, active voice. Examples:

- *Raise rent at [PROPERTY_REF] from £[OLD] / month to £[NEW] / month, effective [DD/MM/YYYY]; Form 4A to be served [DD/MM/YYYY].*
- *Reject application from [APPLICANT_REF] on affordability — gross income £[X] below the 30× rent floor.*
- *Permit one [PET_DESCRIPTION] at [PROPERTY_REF] from [DD/MM/YYYY], with conditions: [CONDITIONS_SUMMARY].*
- *Hold on Section 8 ground 8 service at [PROPERTY_REF] [ROOM_REF]; 14-day informal cure conversation first.*

### PROPERTY / TENANCY
Per-property reference and tenancy reference, in that order. Use the slugs you gave each property (`[PROPERTY-SLUG]`) and each tenancy (`[TENANCY-REF]`) in your systems folder.

### RATIONALE
One to three sentences answering *why this decision and not another*. Reference the evidence inline. Where the rationale is longer than three sentences, write the long-form version into `evidence/[case-rationale].md` and use the rationale field for a one-sentence summary plus link.

For high-stakes decisions (Section 8, complaints, deposit disputes), cite the NRLA helpline call or the solicitor's letter that shaped the decision — the call note template at [`../templates/nrla-helpline-summary.md`](../templates/nrla-helpline-summary.md) gives you the file shape.

### EVIDENCE POINTER
File paths, not the artefacts themselves. The log points to evidence; it does not duplicate it. Examples:

- `evidence/[tenancy]-form-4a-draft-[YYYY-MM].pdf`
- `evidence/[property]-comparable-pull-[YYYY-MM].pdf`
- `evidence/[applicant]-reference-pack-[YYYY].pdf`
- `tenancies/[TENANCY_REF].md (line 16 updated)`
- `properties/[property-short]/maintenance-log.md (entry [N])`

### OUTCOME
Filled when the outcome resolves. Two states:

- *Resolved [DD/MM/YYYY]: [outturn in one or two sentences].*
- *Open as at [DD/MM/YYYY].*

Open entries are flagged at the quarterly bottleneck-finding pass; any open >90 days is a candidate for review.
