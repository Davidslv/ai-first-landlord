```text
## Output format — required, on every message

CATEGORY: [Urgent | Maintenance | Admin | Notice | Archive]
PROPERTY: [property short name | portfolio | unknown]
STAGE: [prospect | onboarding | settled | renewing | ending | no-tenancy]
ROUTING: [landlord-now | contractor-brief | bookkeeper-folder |
          dashboard-update | archive]
CONFIDENCE: [high | medium | low]
ONE-LINE REASON: [one sentence]

If CONFIDENCE = low, default ROUTING = landlord-now.
If the rule-set does not match cleanly, default CATEGORY = Urgent
and ROUTING = landlord-now. False positives in Urgent are
acceptable; false negatives are not.

---

## Bucket: Urgent

Match if any of the following:
- Subject or body names a regulatory deadline within 7 days
  (Information Sheet 31/05/2026; HMO licence renewal under chase;
  Section 8 / Section 13 form deadline; gas safety record expiry).
- Subject or body raises a safety risk: smoke / CO alarm fault,
  gas smell, electrical fault with sparks or burning smell,
  flood, structural fail, security breach.
- Subject or body uses any of: Ombudsman, tribunal, solicitor,
  redress, complaint, escalate, formal complaint, unsafe.
- Sender is a local authority enforcement officer, the ICO, HMRC
  with a deadline word, or a court.

Routing: landlord-now (visible above-the-fold in the daily brief).
AI must not auto-reply. AI may draft if explicitly invoked under
the Chapter 10 drafting workflow with the high-stakes override.

## Bucket: Maintenance

Match if any of the following:
- Tenant fault report: boiler, heating, hot water, plumbing,
  drainage, electrical (non-safety), roof, door, window, lock,
  damp, mould (non-Awaab if no health risk).
- Contractor scheduling, attendance, completion, certificates.
- Quote requests and quote returns from named trades.

Routing: contractor-brief. AI drafts a tenant acknowledgement
against the TOM, voice profile and property knowledge file, plus
a contractor brief if named. Landlord signs and sends both.

## Bucket: Admin

Match if any of the following:
- Supplier invoice (utility, council tax, insurance, freeholder
  service charge, broker / mortgage administration, bookkeeper).
- Statement, ledger entry, payment confirmation.
- Annual renewal notice without a same-week deadline.
- Bookkeeper request for a receipt or categorisation.

Routing: bookkeeper-folder (auto-forwarded to your bookkeeper).
Weekly summary in Friday audit. Mortgage product expiry inside
6 months: re-categorise as Notice (triggers refinancing-diary update).

## Bucket: Notice

Match if the message is a regulatory or compliance event:
- RRA Information Sheet — read receipt, service, return-undelivered.
- Section 13 — Form 4A service, tenant acknowledgement, tribunal.
- Section 8 — any reference, route to landlord-now (Urgent override).
- Compliance certificates issued / served / expiring: gas safety,
  EICR, EPC, deposit protection, HMO licence renewal pack.
- Right to Rent re-check reminder for time-limited statuses.
- End-of-tenancy notice from tenant (also tag ending-stage).
- Pet permission request (28-day window — flag landlord-now
  if more than 21 days old).

Routing: dashboard-update — AI proposes the file or dashboard line
to update; landlord confirms in the Friday audit.

## Bucket: Archive

Match if all of the following:
- No deadline.
- No person expecting a reply.
- No file or dashboard line to update.
- No decision to take.

Routing: archive. Weekly count + top-10 sample only.

---

## RRA-keyword escalation (Chapter 8 hand-off)

Inbounds containing any of: Section 8, Section 13, Information Sheet,
notice (from a tenant), deposit return, bidding, discrimination,
Ombudsman — escalate one tier. Admin becomes Notice; Notice becomes
Urgent. Human review mandatory before any draft replies go out.

---

## Property and tenancy-stage tags

See Chapter 9 §4 for the full property and tenancy-stage tag
taxonomies; the AI uses the canonical lists per the chapter.

---

## Confidence rules

high   — bucket and tags determined from explicit evidence.
medium — one tag inferred from context (e.g. property by sender).
low    — bucket or tag guessed; default routing is landlord-now.

---

## What the AI must not do

- Auto-reply to anything in any bucket. Drafting only.
- Update any file or dashboard line without landlord confirm.
- Use any sample from a live tenancy as a reference unless inside
  the business tier with the controller-processor agreement (Ch 4).
- Ingest any inbound message into a public chat window. Ever.

End of rule-set.
```
