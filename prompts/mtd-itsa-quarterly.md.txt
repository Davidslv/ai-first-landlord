```text
You are drafting a quarterly MTD ITSA extract for a UK landlord in
cohort 1 (qualifying income above £50,000, live since 6 April 2026).
Two reportable streams under MTD ITSA Phase 1: a personal-name
property co-owned 50/50 with a spouse (50% share of gross rent and
allowable expenses); a sole-trader consultancy (reportable in full).
[N] further properties sit in a limited-company SPV that files
Corporation Tax separately and is OUT OF SCOPE for this extract.

INPUTS
- [Bookkeeping software] ledger, YTD [TAX_YEAR_START] to [QUARTER_END],
  with bank-feed transactions categorised, receipts attached, journals
  digitally linked.
- [Bank] personal current (joint) and business current (sole)
  closing balances at [QUARTER_END] for bank reconciliation.
- The daily categorisation rule-set, mapped to HMRC's MTD ITSA
  quarterly fields.

OUTPUT — one markdown page, two streams, year-to-date figures,
in the order below. Sections by HMRC category — see Chapter 15 §3.

- Header: tax-payer, UTR, NI, tax year, Q dates, deadline (7th
  of the second month after Q end), source software, generation
  timestamp, reviewer (your bookkeeper), sign-off (the landlord),
  submitter (the bookkeeper via ASA).
- PROPERTY STREAM: tenancy ref, Q window, INCOME (gross rent x
  months; 50% share as reportable line), EXPENSES by HMRC
  category with 50% share applied at line level, NET PROPERTY
  (Q cumulative — Q1 = Q1; Q2 = Q1+Q2; etc.).
- SELF-EMPLOYMENT STREAM: trade, Q window, INCOME, EXPENSES by
  HMRC category, NET SELF-EMPLOYMENT (Q cumulative).
- CUMULATIVE YEAR-TO-DATE TOTALS — net property + net
  self-employment.
- BANK RECONCILIATION — ledger balance vs statement balance at
  [QUARTER_END]; flag breaks, do not silently absorb.
- DIGITAL-LINKS COMPLIANCE — every line bank-feed sourced with
  receipt; no figure re-keyed between systems.
- QUERIES FOR [BOOKKEEPER] — every Q-FOR-[BOOKKEEPER] line:
  amount, AI's categorisation, the alternative the bookkeeper may
  prefer.
- SUBMISSION CHAIN — table of dates: AI draft, bookkeeper
  cross-check, landlord sign-off, bookkeeper submits via ASA,
  HMRC receipt, deadline.

CONSTRAINTS
- Draft only — footer says "Do not file."
- Use only figures in the ledger and bank-feed reconciliation.
  Do not estimate. Missing figures surface as Q-FOR-[BOOKKEEPER].
- Apply the 50% co-ownership split at the quarterly extract step,
  not at the bank-feed line.
- Cumulative reporting — Q[N] is YTD [tax year start] through Q end.
- No SPV transactions. If a personal account evidences SPV
  income or expense, flag for an intercompany journal to the
  SPV's ledger; do not categorise it for MTD ITSA.
```
