## OVERRIDES

If the schedule of dilapidations was **not signed at the walk** (tenant disputed deductions, walk happened in the tenant's absence, deductions still being scoped against contractor quotes), do not fire this template. Manual draft, naming the open items and the timeline for resolution.

If the tenant is **disputing the deduction** post-walk, do not fire this template. Route to the Chapter 10 high-stakes override; the dispute resolution is the deposit scheme's free service (TDS, DPS, MyDeposits all run an adjudication on the evidence — the schedule of dilapidations, the move-in inventory, photographic record). The schedule itself is the load-bearing artefact; this email is only a cover.

If the deposit is **larger than the scheme certificate amount** (top-up paid mid-tenancy, never re-protected), surface the discrepancy in `decision-log/complaints.md` before sending. The protection-cap rules (5 weeks under £50,000/year rent under the Tenant Fees Act 2019) constrain what can be returned through the scheme.
