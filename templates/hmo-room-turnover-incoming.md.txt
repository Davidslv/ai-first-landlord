## OVERRIDES

If the deposit has **not yet been received** by the date you would normally send this email, hold the template — the deposit-protection clock starts on receipt and the email's deposit reference would mislead.

If the incoming sharer is **moving in mid-tenancy of a household with active issues** (noise complaint live, recent room turnover under dispute, fire-safety remedial ongoing), drop the *quick household intro* line — household introductions need separate handling that does not pre-empt the substantive issues.

If the property is **not yet on a current HMO licence** at the date of move-in (renewal in train, retrospective application pending), do not state the licence-holder-pays-council-tax line as fact — name it as the standing arrangement subject to licence confirmation.
