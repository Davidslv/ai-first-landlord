Subject: Renters' Rights Act 2025 — Information Sheet for [PROPERTY_REF]

Hi [TENANT_FIRST_NAME(S)],

Attached is the **Renters' Rights Act 2025 Information Sheet**
published by gov.uk in March 2026. Under the Act, every existing
tenancy in place before 1 May 2026 needs to receive this sheet —
deadline 31 May 2026. I'm serving it now to keep us inside that
window.

In short:

  - Your tenancy converted automatically from an Assured Shorthold
    Tenancy to an **Assured Periodic Tenancy** on 1 May 2026. No
    new agreement, no fixed term — the tenancy continues on the
    terms of your existing agreement, periodic from now on.
  - You can give two months' notice to end at any time.
  - Section 21 (the old "no-fault" notice) has been abolished.
    Section 8 still exists, with expanded grounds.
  - Rent can only be raised via Section 13, once per twelve months,
    with two months' notice and a tenant right to challenge at the
    First-tier Tribunal.
  - Pet requests must be considered and decided within 28 days,
    refused only on reasonable grounds.

The full sheet has all of this in the gov.uk plain-English form;
please open it before [READ_BY_DATE] so I have a read-receipt on
file.

Nothing for you to do — your tenancy carries on as before.

[LANDLORD_FIRST_NAME]

— Attached: gov.uk Information Sheet 2026 (PDF, version-pinned)

## OVERRIDES

**Do not fire this template** if any of the following:

- The tenancy started **on or after 1 May 2026** — use [`written-statement-cover.md.txt`](written-statement-cover.md.txt) instead. The Information Sheet does not apply to new lets.
- The tenant has explicitly requested **postal service** — do not email; deliver by recorded post or in person with a signed receipt.
- A complaint heading toward redress is open on this tenancy — record service via the high-stakes override path with NRLA helpline / solicitor sight before sending.

**Version-pin the gov.uk PDF** — file the dated version (e.g. *info-sheet-2026-gov-uk-20-03-2026.pdf*) so the audit trail shows which version was served on which date.
