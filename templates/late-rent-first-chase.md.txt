The [PAYMENT_PERIOD] rent didn't come through on the [RENT_DAY] —

## OVERRIDES

If the tenant has **two-plus months' arrears** (over the Section 8 ground 8 mandatory threshold for monthly rent), do not fire this template. Drop to [`section-8-ground-8-first-chase.md.txt`](section-8-ground-8-first-chase.md.txt) and route through the Chapter 10 high-stakes override — solicitor or NRLA legal helpline review before any Form 3A is contemplated.

If the tenant is in a **payment dispute** that has previously been escalated (deposit dispute, complaint heading toward redress), do not fire this template. Manual draft, with the audit-trail rationale recorded in the decision log under the `complaints` category.

If the tenant has a **prior payment-history flag** in TOM line 17 (one-off explained delay; bank-error pattern), the template still fires but soften the *I'm sure it's something operational* phrasing if it would read as patronising on a third repeat.
