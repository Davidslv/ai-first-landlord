## OVERRIDES

If the property is an **HMO under licence**, fire this template at the standard 8-week chase **and** check the documents-to-refresh chase (12-week pre-pack — see [`../dashboard/chase-rhythm.md`](../dashboard/chase-rhythm.md)). The HMO licence renewal pack needs the refreshed CP12 alongside refreshed EICR and fire-system service certificate.

If the contractor on file is **unavailable** for the slot window, do not draft with `[CONTRACTOR_NAME]` blank. Name the standby explicitly — *Streatham Gas standing in this year while [primary contractor] is rebuilding their winter capacity*.

If the tenant has previously **refused access** in a way that risked the previous year's CP12 cycle, drop the template and ring. The Chapter 10 high-stakes override applies — gas safety access has a separate route under s.11 LTA 1985 and the gas safety regulations, and a refusal pattern is a decision-log item under `decision-log/maintenance.md`.

If the boiler is **out of warranty and showing a fault pattern in the maintenance log** (TOM line 15), fire the template but flag for the landlord to consider whether to commission a separate diagnostic on top of the annual service.
