## OVERRIDES

**Do not fire this template** if any of the following:

- The tenancy has had a Section 13 rent review served within the last twelve months (RRA s.7 once-per-twelve-months restriction). Surface in the dashboard as *out of cycle*.
- A First-tier Tribunal challenge to a previous Section 13 is open or unresolved on this tenancy.
- A tenant complaint heading toward redress is open.
- The proposed rent is **above the open-market range** for comparable lets — manual draft, NRLA helpline call recorded in `decision-log/s13-rent-reviews.md`, solicitor sign-off if challenged.
- The tenancy is within the post-grant 52-week window for a new RRA-Phase-1 let. Earliest effective date for a first Section 13 is 52 weeks after grant.

**Form 4A itself is not drafted by the AI.** Use the gov.uk-published current version. The AI drafts only the cover note above, with the Form 4A as an attachment.
