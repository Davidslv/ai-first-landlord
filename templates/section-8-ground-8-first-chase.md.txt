## OVERRIDES — Chapter 10 high-stakes

**This template requires the high-stakes override pass:**

1. **Log the NRLA helpline call** in [`../decision-log/`](../decision-log/) under `s8-possession.md` before sending.
2. **Solicitor on standby** — name your property solicitor and a planned consultation date if the cure window passes without resolution.
3. **The Form 3A is human-signed.** The AI does not draft the Form 3A itself. Statutory grounds wording is solicitor/NRLA-reviewed.
4. **Three-question review pass is mandatory** even on the cover email — factual (arrears figure agrees with the rent ledger?); tonal (firm but not threatening?); compliance (Section 8 ground 8 threshold actually crossed? not Section 21 language?).

**Hold the template** if:

- The tenant has a pending hardship claim (universal credit transition, payroll dispute named with employer reference, named back-pay date inside 14 days).
- A previous late-payment chase from this tenant resulted in a payment plan that is current and being honoured.
- The tenancy has a prior complaint heading toward redress that the arrears claim might intersect.

In any of these cases, manual draft against the decision-log rationale; solicitor or NRLA helpline review before sending.
