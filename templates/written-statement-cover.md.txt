## OVERRIDES

**Do not fire this template** if any of the following:

- The tenancy started **before 1 May 2026** — use [`information-sheet-cover.md.txt`](information-sheet-cover.md.txt) instead.
- The tenancy agreement has **not yet been solicitor-reviewed** at first portfolio use of the template — the AI does not draft the agreement; the cover email assumes the underlying contract is sound.
- The tenant has **not yet completed referencing** — service of the Written Statement is part of the signing flow, not the marketing-and-viewings flow.
- A holding deposit is held but **the full deposit has not yet been received** — do not commit a key-handover time the deposit will not have cleared by.

**Right to Rent must be on file before the tenancy is granted.** This is a separate landlord-owned step; the cover email above does not refer to it because the check is complete by the time the email is sent.
